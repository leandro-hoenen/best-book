FAQ

What you can expect from this book marketplace?

As a Buyer:
- With several filter options, the buyer can find the book she/he is looking for.
- Each book offered has a detailed description to help the buyer decide wether it is the right book or not.
- The buyer can contact the seller by email to buy the book.

As a seller:
- Sellers can register on the website to post their offers.
- Sellers can edit their profile information to increase their credibility.
- For sellers there is an overview where they can see how many potential buyers have checked out their books.

Account Administration
- Both buyers and sellers can create an account. For buyers, this option is optional. Sellers need to create an account to sell their books.
- Account data such as username, email address, the offers, phone number or password are editable.
- Password can be reset.

How can I contact the seller?

For each book offered there is an email address from the seller on the description page. You can click directly on the email address and contact the seller.

Do I need to create an account a a buyer?

No, you don't need to create an account as a buyer. Creating an account as a buyer is optional.

How can I pay for the book?

Payment methods you can arrange directly with the seller.

For how long can I publish the offer as a seller?

You can keep the offer until you find a buyer for your book.

Can I deactivate my account?

You can deactivate or delete your account if you no longer wish to be a member of the marketplace.
