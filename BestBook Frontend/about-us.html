About Us

First of all, a warm welcome to your main marketplace for books to buy and sell. We are four business information students inspired by the idea of saving the environment and acting like superheroes. Well, we won't save the whole world, but at least we will save your wallet from spending way too much money on books for your studies. We've created a platform where new students can buy used books from older semesters. So it's a win-win situation - older semesters don't have to throw away books - so you earn a little money and save the environment.
What more do you want!
